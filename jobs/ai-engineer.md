# AI Engineer

## Role summary
We are looking for an AI Engineer to design, build, and deploy ML models and AI-powered features—including LLMs, embeddings, and evaluation pipelines—in production.

## Must-have requirements
- 3+ years of software or ML engineering experience
- Strong Python and experience with ML frameworks (e.g. PyTorch, TensorFlow, or Hugging Face)
- Experience shipping ML models or AI features to production
- Familiarity with LLMs, prompt engineering, RAG, or fine-tuning
- Clear written and verbal communication

## Nice-to-have
- Experience with vector DBs, embeddings, or evaluation frameworks
- MLOps (training pipelines, monitoring, versioning)
- Research or publications in ML/NLP

## What we value
- Rigorous evaluation and iteration on model behavior
- Collaboration with product and research
- Pragmatic trade-offs between quality, latency, and cost
