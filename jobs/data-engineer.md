# Data Engineer

## Role summary
We are looking for a Data Engineer to build and maintain data pipelines, warehouses, and tooling that power analytics and ML across the company.

## Must-have requirements
- 3+ years of data engineering or backend experience
- Strong SQL and experience with a data warehouse (Snowflake, BigQuery, Redshift, or similar)
- Experience building ETL/ELT pipelines (batch or streaming)
- Proficiency in Python or Scala for data workloads
- Clear written and verbal communication

## Nice-to-have
- Experience with dbt, Airflow, or similar orchestration
- Spark or other distributed processing
- ML pipelines or feature stores

## What we value
- Data quality and reliability
- Collaboration with analytics and product
- Pragmatic scaling and performance
