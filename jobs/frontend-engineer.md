# Frontend Engineer

## Role summary
We are looking for a Frontend Engineer to build and maintain our web applications, design systems, and user-facing features with a focus on accessibility and performance.

## Must-have requirements
- 3+ years of frontend development experience
- Strong JavaScript/TypeScript and modern React (or equivalent framework)
- Experience with responsive design and CSS
- Understanding of accessibility (a11y) and performance
- Clear written and verbal communication

## Nice-to-have
- Experience with Next.js, GraphQL, or state management
- Design systems or component libraries
- Testing (unit, integration, e2e)

## What we value
- User experience and craft
- Collaboration with design and product
- Maintainable and scalable frontend architecture
