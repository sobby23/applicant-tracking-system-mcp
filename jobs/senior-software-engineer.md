# Senior Software Engineer

## Role summary
We are looking for a Senior Software Engineer to build and maintain backend services and APIs.

## Must-have requirements
- 3+ years of professional software development experience
- Strong experience in Python or similar language
- Experience with REST APIs and web services
- Experience with databases (SQL or NoSQL)
- Clear written and verbal communication

## Nice-to-have
- Experience with cloud platforms (e.g. AWS, GCP)
- Experience with async/event-driven systems
- Open source or side projects

## What we value
- Ownership and collaboration
- Pragmatic trade-offs and shipping
- Empathy for users and teammates
