# Product Manager

## Role summary
We are looking for a Product Manager to own the roadmap for our B2B platform, work with engineering and design, and drive outcomes through data and user research.

## Must-have requirements
- 3+ years of product management experience
- Experience defining PRDs, roadmaps, and success metrics
- Strong collaboration with engineering and design
- Data-driven decision making (analytics, A/B tests, user research)
- Clear written and verbal communication

## Nice-to-have
- Experience in B2B or SaaS
- Technical background (e.g. prior engineering or CS degree)
- Experience with agile or scrum

## What we value
- Customer obsession and empathy
- Bias for action and ownership
- Clarity in prioritization and trade-offs
